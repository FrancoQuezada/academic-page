# Franco Quezada — Academic Personal Webpage

Personal academic website for **Franco Antonio Quezada Valenzuela**, Assistant Professor & Research Associate at the Universidad de Santiago de Chile (USACH), Faculty of Engineering.

**Live site:** [franco.quezada@usach.cl](mailto:franco.quezada@usach.cl)

## About

Static single-page academic portfolio covering research, publications, projects, teaching, and awards. No frameworks or build tools — just plain HTML, CSS, and minimal vanilla JavaScript.

**Research areas:** Stochastic Programming · Operations Research · Circular Economy & Remanufacturing · Sustainable Energy Systems · Healthcare Operations · Environmental Optimization
