Focus Areas

Research Interests

01

Stochastic Programming

Multi-stage stochastic integer programming, SDDIP algorithms, and risk-averse models for production planning and inventory management under uncertainty.

02

Circular Economy & Remanufacturing

Lot-sizing models with returns and remanufacturing, extended producer responsibility, and sustainable supply chain design under uncertainty.

03

Sustainable Energy Systems

Stochastic optimization for local energy markets, peer-to-peer energy trading, and collective self-consumption with renewable energy integration.

04

Operations Research

Combinatorial optimization, Benders decomposition, polyhedral approaches, and exact methods for scheduling, routing, and districting problems.

05

Healthcare Operations

Multi-appointment scheduling for oncology patients, healthcare districting, and resource allocation optimization in medical service delivery.

06

Environmental Optimization

Stochastic programming for firebreak placement, recyclable solid waste collection network design, and environmental risk management.
